Create a new game called Velocity.

Concept:
Velocity is a swipe-to-dodge survival game.

The player controls a glowing orb in the center of the screen.

Obstacles spawn from one of four directions:
- top
- bottom
- left
- right

The player must swipe in the opposite direction to dodge.

Example:
Obstacle from left → swipe right
Obstacle from top → swipe down

If correct:
- obstacle avoided
- score increases
- combo increases
- particle burst plays
- success haptic

If wrong:
- player loses a life
- combo resets
- error flash + haptic
