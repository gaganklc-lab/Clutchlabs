FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We have answers.

Is ClutchTap free to play?
Yes, ClutchTap is completely free. All power-ups are earned through gameplay, not purchases.
What platforms does it run on?
ClutchTap runs on iOS, Android (via Expo Go), and web browsers. Scan the QR code on our homepage to get started on mobile.
How do I unlock tile themes?
Tile themes unlock as you level up by earning XP. There are 6 themes that unlock at levels 3, 5, 8, 12, 16, and 20.
How does the daily challenge work?
Every day a unique rule sequence is generated from a daily seed. Everyone gets the same challenge, so you can compare scores with friends.
Is my data stored online?
No. All your scores, progress, and settings are stored locally on your device. We don't collect any personal data.
How do I earn power-ups?
Shield is earned every 5 games, Time Freeze on 5+ combo streaks, and Double Points every 500 XP. They accumulate up to 3 of each type.
